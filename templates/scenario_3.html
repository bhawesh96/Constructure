Option 2: Inject chemical solutions, like silicates of soda and calcium chloride with pressure into the soil. These chemical along with the soil particles form a gel like structure and develop a compact mass. This gives additional strength to soft soils at deeper depths.

Option 3: Compact the soil by ramming or rolling, thus increasing its density and shear strength. As a result the bearing capacity of soil also increases.

Option 4: Leave the soil as it is, and risk it.
