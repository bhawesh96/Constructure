Rules

Construction shall take place over the course of 6 stages. Each stage revolves around a particular activity that can be performed in multiple ways. Not all of these options are optimal. You will be judged on how well your choices work together.

Over the course of each round, you will earn Gold Dragons, the currency of Westeros, based on how many multiple choice questions you have answered correctly. At the end of a round, you will use these Gold Dragons to select an option from those you can afford.

Note that these coins do not carry forward to the next round. If you have not used up all the Gold Dragons while selecting an option, the remainder will enhance your judgement score.

Rules

Starting from Round 2, there will be a rapid-fire bonus round at the end of each stage. The questions maybe image prompted, riddles, or word scrambles. The number of questions you can answer in the stipulated time bestow extra rewards. Make note that you cannot log out during the rapid fire round. Doing so would result in you losing the extra Gold Dragons you may have earned.

If, at the end of a given round, you do not have enough currency to buy even the cheapest option available to you, then the round can be restarted. Be warned however, that you only have 3 retries. Our Queen does not take kindly to failing too many times.
